Kirk Lazarus
Uncharted 4

Ladies and gentlemen, if I, Kirk Lazarus, were to review a game, it'd be like comparing method acting to a thumb war with a kitten. ... Now, imagine "The Witcher 3" meets "Grand Theft Auto," sprinkled with the drama of a Shakespearean monologue -- that's what we've got here. But remember, in this game, there's no "cut" button, so don't be surprised if you find yourself talking to NPCs longer than you've ever talked to your therapist.
